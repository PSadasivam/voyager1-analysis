PWS Instrument:
The Plasma Wave Subsystem measures electric field fluctuations from 10 Hz to 56.2 kHz using a 10-m dipole antenna. It detects Langmuir waves, whistler-mode emissions, and electron plasma oscillations critical for probing the interstellar medium.
